Parameters?

Parameters are what you define when you write a function or sub procedure: they are defined by name in parentheses after the procedure name, and for each parameter, the data type, passing mechanism (by value of reference) - and for some languages whether they are optional - are defined.


and Arguments?

Arguments are what are specified when you use a function or sub in a calling program, and can consist of variable names, constants and functions.
